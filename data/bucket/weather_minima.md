## Section 1: General VFR Minima
All student solo and dual flights require VFR conditions at minimum.

**Ceiling:** 1500 ft AGL minimum for training flights.
**Visibility:** 5 SM minimum for all flight training.
**Wind:** Max crosswind 15 kt for dual; 10 kt for solo flights.

## Section 2: Minima by Stage

### PPL Stage 1-2 (Early Training)
- Ceiling: 2500 ft AGL
- Visibility: 8 SM
- Wind: 10 kt max, crosswind 8 kt max
- Sortie types: CIRCUITS only

### PPL Stage 3-4 (Advanced)
- Ceiling: 1500 ft AGL
- Visibility: 5 SM
- Wind: 15 kt max, crosswind 12 kt max
- Sortie types: CIRCUITS, NAV, CHK_PREP

### Solo Flights (any stage)
- Ceiling: 3000 ft AGL
- Visibility: 10 SM
- Wind: 10 kt max, crosswind 8 kt max

## Section 3: SIM Training
- SIM_PROCEDURES can proceed in any weather (ground-based).
- SIM sessions require no weather minima check.

## Section 4: NO-GO Conditions
Any of the following triggers an automatic NO-GO:
- Ceiling below stage minima
- Visibility below stage minima
- Thunderstorm within 10 NM
- Wind exceeding stage limits
